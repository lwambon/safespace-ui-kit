- Communities seeking safer digital spaces

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is SafeSpace really anonymous?**  
A: Yes! When you choose anonymous reporting, absolutely no personal information is shared. We use end-to-end encryption to protect your data.

**Q: Does it work without internet?**  
A: Yes! You can create reports offline. They'll automatically sync when you're back online.

**Q: Is SafeSpace free?**  
A: Completely free. Always. No hidden costs or premium features.

**Q: How long does report review take?**  
A: Most reports are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Urgent cases are prioritized and may be reviewed within hours.

**Q: Can I report on behalf of someone else?**  
A: Yes. Indicate in the report that you're submitting on someone's behalf and provide their consent if possible.

### Safety Questions

**Q: Can the harasser know I reported them?**  
A: Not through SafeSpace. Your identity is protected unless you explicitly choose to share it.

**Q: What happens to my evidence?**  
A: It's encrypted and stored securely on protected servers. Only authorized personnel can access it for your specific case.

**Q: Can I delete my reports?**  
A: Yes, you have full control. However, we recommend keeping them for potential legal proceedings.

**Q: Will SafeSpace contact authorities?**  
A: Only with your explicit permission. You decide if and when to involve law enforcement.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which platforms does SafeSpace monitor?**  
A: You can report incidents from any platform (Instagram, Twitter, WhatsApp, TikTok, etc.). We document the evidence you provide.

**Q: How does the AI detection work?**  
A: Our AI analyzes text patterns for harmful language, understanding context and cultural nuances. It's trained on diverse African language datasets.

**Q: Is my data shared with third parties?**  
A: Never. We don't sell your data. Aggregated, anonymous statistics may be shared with researchers and policymakers to improve safety policies.

**Q: What languages does SafeSpace support?**  
A: Currently English, Swahili, and French, with more African languages coming soon.

### Account Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**  
A: Tap "Forgot Password?" on the login screen. You'll receive a reset link via email.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Yes, go to Settings â†’ Account â†’ Change Email.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Settings â†’ Account â†’ Delete Account. Note: This is permanent and cannot be undone.
